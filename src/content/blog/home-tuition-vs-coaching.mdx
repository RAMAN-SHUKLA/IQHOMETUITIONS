Many students in Kanpur spend hours traveling to coaching hubs like Kakadeo. But is it always the best option?

## Travel Time and Fatigue
Home tuition saves valuable travel time, allowing students to use that energy for actual self-study.

## Safe and Comfortable Environment
Learning at home provides a familiar environment where students feel more comfortable asking questions.
