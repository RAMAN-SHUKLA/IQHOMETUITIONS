## 1. Identify Your Child's Specific Needs
Before starting your search, understand precisely where your child needs help. 
* Are they struggling with advanced mathematical concepts in Class 12?
* Do they need foundational help in science? 
* Are they preparing for competitive exams like JEE or NEET?

**Fact:** According to educational records in Uttar Pradesh, students who receive subject-specific tutoring show a **35% higher retention rate** compared to generalized tutoring.

## 2. Check for Qualifications and Experience
A good tutor should not only know the subject but also know *how* to teach it. 
Look for tutors with a strong academic background from reputable institutions in Kanpur, such as:
- **IIT Kanpur** (Indian Institute of Technology)
- **HBTI / HBTU** (Harcourt Butler Technical University)
- **CSJM University**

Tutors from these universities have gone through rigorous academic trials themselves and know the exact strategies needed to succeed.
