Kanpur is a vast city with distinct educational hubs. Here are the top 5 areas where home tuition is in high demand and tutors are readily available:

1. **Kidwai Nagar**: A major residential hub with top-tier schools.
2. **Swaroop Nagar**: Known for its premium educational environment.
3. **Kakadeo**: The coaching capital of Kanpur, now shifting towards home tutoring.
4. **Civil Lines**: Centrally located with access to expert professionals.
5. **Indira Nagar**: A rapidly growing area for quality education.
